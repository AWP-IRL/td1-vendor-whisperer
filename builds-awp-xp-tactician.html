Build Guide

I primarily use this build for open world (Light Zone) boss runs but also for Hard and Challenging missions and Underground runs. With this build, you can BFB one-shot kill all bosses except the Heavies (i.e. Soretto, Chains, and Jacks).

The focus of this build is to maximize XP rewards, thus increasing the number of Field Proficiency or Underground caches, and your opportunities for Classified or Exotic items. The primary weapon, Lightweight M4, is for shooting, while the secondary weapon, LVOA-C, is your stat stick for maximizing damage while using your skills. The LVOA-C can be used for shooting when in a bind but the Lightweight M4 does more damage. The off hand weapon, Double Barrel Sawed Off, is used to maximize the Survivor link in order to go fast longer while running from boss to boss. With the Sawed Off in your hand, throw out your Cluster seeker mines, and just before they impact the NPCs you pop your Survivor link.
