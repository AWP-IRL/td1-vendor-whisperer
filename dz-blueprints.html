Blueprints available at Dark Zone vendors

This may come as a surprise to some but there are a total of 60 Specialized and Superior rarity blueprints available for sale at DZ vendors between levels 6 and 29. Unfortunately, most of them are of little value and the additional Specialized Division Tech crafting material cost makes them less desirable. The one possible exception is the level 26 Superior Custom PF45 blueprint which can be used to craft and recalibrate into Coolheaded and Toxic pistol. However, if you are a completionist like me then you will want to collect them.

These blueprints are available at most Dark Zone safe rooms, as well as the Dark Zone checkpoints at Park Avenue, E 58th St, E 61st St, and E 64th St. The type of blueprint that appears at a vendor aligns with their vendor type (e.g. the DZ gear vendors at the DZ02 and DZ08 safe room, and E 58th St checkpoint sell gear blueprints) and all vendors of the same type sell the same blueprints. There is no weekly rotation to the blueprints. For speed and safety, it is best if you can unlock the checkpoint vendors as you can fast travel to them and they sell all of the blueprints.

These blueprints have a DZ rank requirement in order to purchase, which starts at rank 10 and later requires rank 15. As the first blueprints disappear after level 15, I suggest going into the Dark Zone at or before level 14, which is the highest level in the first Dark Zone bracket (1 to 14), in order to rank up for these requirements.
